# AI SalonOS — Product Requirements Document

## Vision

Build an **AI Employee** that works 24/7 for appointment-based businesses (salons, spas, clinics). It acts as receptionist, sales executive, marketer, and analyst — all in one.

## Phase 1 — MVP Scope

### Core Capabilities

1. **AI Receptionist** — understands customer requests and responds naturally via WhatsApp, website chat, Instagram, Facebook
2. **Smart Appointment Engine** — checks availability, books, reschedules, cancels appointments
3. **Customer CRM** — auto-creates and updates customer profiles from every interaction
4. **Owner Dashboard** — today's appointments, leads, revenue, customer metrics

### Target Users

- **Primary:** Salon/spa owners and managers
- **Secondary:** Front-desk staff and stylists
- **Tertiary:** Customers booking appointments

### Success Metrics

- Appointment booking time < 30 seconds via AI
- Zero double-bookings
- Customer profile auto-creation from first message
- Dashboard load time < 2 seconds
- AI response time < 3 seconds

## Future Phases

- Phase 2: Marketing automation, loyalty, review collection
- Phase 3: AI business insights, churn prediction, smart upselling
- Phase 4: Voice AI, multi-branch, white-label SaaS
